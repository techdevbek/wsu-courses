\begin{enumerate}

\item Find an orthonormal basis for $\mathbb{P}_3$ with respect to the inner product $$\langle f,g\rangle=\int_{0}^{1} f g\ dx$$

\item Let $\beta$ be a basis for a subspace $W$ of an inner product space $V$. Prove that $z\in W^\perp$ if and only if $\langle z,v\rangle=0\ \forall v\in \beta$.

\item Let $W$ be a finite-dimensional subspace of an inner product space $V$. Prove that
$V=W\bigoplus W^\perp$. 


\item Let $W_1$ and $W_2$ be subspaces of a finite-dimensional inner product space. Prove that
$(W_1+W_2)^\perp=W_1^\perp\cap W_2^\perp$ and $(W_1\cap W_2)^\perp=W_1^\perp+W_2^\perp$.
